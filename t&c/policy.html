BotsApp Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how BotsApp collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our service. By using BotsApp, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly, such as:

  • Account information: Username, email address, password (hashed), full name, gender, about (bio), profile picture.
  • Chat information: Messages, files, images, and emojis you share.
  • Group chat information: Group names, member lists, and group profile pictures.
  • User actions: Chat requests, blocking actions, reporting actions.

We also collect information automatically when you use our service, such as:

  • Usage data: Information about how you use BotsApp, such as the features you access, the messages you send, and the time you spend in the application.
  • Device information: Your device type, operating system, and browser information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our service.
  • Process your chat requests and messages.
  • Manage your account and profile.
  • Send you notifications and updates.
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends.
  • Protect the security of our service.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Third-party service providers: We may use third-party service providers to assist us in providing and improving our service. These providers may have access to your information to perform services on our behalf, such as hosting our servers, analyzing usage data, or providing customer support.
  • Law enforcement: We may disclose your information to law enforcement if required by law or if we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of our users or the public.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your information: You can request to see the information we have collected about you.
  • Correct your information: You can request to correct any inaccurate information we have about you.
  • Delete your information: You can request to delete your account and the information associated with it.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no website or online service can guarantee complete security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
