BotsApp User Help Guide

Navigating BotsApp

BotsApp is designed to be user-friendly. Here's a quick guide to navigating the main sections:

  • Sidebar: The sidebar provides access to the main features of BotsApp:
    • Personal: View and manage your personal chats.
    • Group: View and manage your group chats.
    • Admin Panel (if you are admin): Access administrative features.
    • Add New Chat: Create a new chat (personal or group).
    • Notifications: View and manage your notifications.
    • Settings: Customize your account settings, theme, and wallpaper.
    • Profile: View and edit your profile information.
  • Chat List: The chat list displays all your active conversations. Click on a chat to open it.
  • Chat Window: The chat window displays messages, attachments, and options for interacting with the chat.

Sending Messages

To send a message:

  1. Open the chat window with the recipient (personal) or group you want to message.
  2. Type your message in the text area at the bottom of the chat window.
  3. Click the "Send" button (or press Enter) to send the message.

Sharing Files and Images

To share files and images:

  1. Open the chat window with the recipient (personal) or group you want to share with.
  2. Click the "Send Documents" button in the chat footer.
  3. Select the file or image you want to share.
  4. A progress bar will display while the file is uploading.

To download a shared file or image:

  1. Click the download button that appears next to the file or image in the chat window.

Using Custom Emojis

To use custom emojis:

  1. Click the "Add Emoji" button in a group chat or the "Upload Emoji" button in the general settings.
  2. Select an emoji from your list or upload a new emoji.
  3. Use the uploaded emoji in your messages.

Chat Requests

To send a chat request to a new user:

  1. Type the username of the user you want to chat with in the search bar.
  2. Click the "Send Request" button.

To accept or decline a chat request:

  1. You will receive a notification when you have a new chat request.
  2. Click on the notification to view the request.
  3. Click "Accept" to start chatting with the user or "Decline" to reject the request.

Group Chat Management

To create a new group chat:

  1. Click the "Add New Chat" button in the sidebar.
  2. Select "Group" from the options.
  3. Enter a name for your group and select the friends you want to add.
  4. Click "Create Group" to create the group.

To manage a group chat:

  1. Open the group chat window.
  2. Click the group's profile picture to open the group profile.
  3. From the group profile, you can add or remove members, change the group name, and delete the group if you are the admin.

User Profile Management

To view and edit your profile:

  1. Click your profile picture in the sidebar.
  2. You can view your username, full name, gender, about (bio), email, and online status settings.
  3. Click the "Edit" icon next to any field to change its value.

Notifications

BotsApp uses notifications to keep you informed about new messages, chat requests, and other important events. You can view and manage your notifications by clicking the "Notifications" button in the sidebar.
